🔍 Book 6–8 Weeks Out

Sweet spot for budget airlines. Prices spike inside 3 weeks and often drop after 6 months.

📆 Fly Tuesday or Wednesday

Midweek flights are 15–25% cheaper than Friday and Sunday. Set alerts for these days.

🌧️ Shoulder Season is Gold

Apr–May and Sep–Oct: near-perfect weather at 30–40% lower than peak prices.

Core Principles

The 6 Rules of Budget Travel

01

Plan the budget before the destination

Know your number first, then find the destination that fits it — not the other way around.

02

Travel slowly, spend less

Movement costs money. Staying longer in one place cuts costs dramatically and gives a richer experience.

03

Free experiences beat paid ones

The best memories rarely have price tags — hiking, markets, local friendships, neighborhoods.

04

Your home expenses travel with you

Subscriptions, phone plans, and commitments back home eat your travel budget before you even leave.

05

Income doesn't have to stay home

Remote work and freelancing have changed everything. Chapter 9 breaks down exactly how to start.

06

The trip that doesn't happen costs the most

The money you save by waiting is outweighed by experiences, connections, and growth you miss.
